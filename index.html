Resources

Resources available in our enviroment whcih can be used to satisfy our needs provided, it is technologically accesible, economically feasible, & culturally acceptable can be termed as RESOURCES.

Resources can be divided as

We can classify Resources also thorugh

Origin
1.Biotic Resourcs Resourcs which are obtained by nature and are living are Biotic Resources.
2.Abiotic Resourcs Resourcs which are obtained by non-living things and are non-living are Abiotic Resources.

Exhaustiblity
1.Renewable Resourcs The Resources which can be renewed or reproduced by physical , chemical or mechanical process is calledRenewable Resources.
2.Non-renewable Resourcs These occur over very long geological time , mineral and fossil fules are the examples.These Resources take millions of year in their formation and are called Non-renewable Resources.

Ownership
1.Indivisual Resourcs These are owned by indivisual person
2.community Resourcs These are resources accesible to community people.
3.National Resourcs Controlled by nation.The country has legal power to even acquire even private property for the public goods.
Land within the political boundry and oceanic area up to 22.2km from the coast is termed as territorial water and resource which belong to the nation.
4.InterNational Resourcs The oceanic resource beyond 200km of the exclusive economic zone belongs to open ocean and no indivisual country can utilize these without concurrence of international istituion.

Status Of Development
1.potential resourcs Resources which are found in a region, but have not been utilised
Ex-The western part of country particularly Rajasthan and Gujrat for development of wind and solar engery but so far these nat been developed properly..
2.Developed resources Resources which are surveyed and their quality have been determined for utilisation.
The development of resources of resources depends on technology and level of their feasibility.
3.Stock Material in the enviroment which have the potential to satisfy human needs but human being do not have the appropriate technology to acess them.
4.Reserves Reserves and the subset of the stocks which can be put into use with the help of existing 'know-how' but their use has not been started.These can be used for meeting future requirement.
